Hello, This is Maram

Maram is a UX designer with a background in front-end development . In 2023 , She graduated from the University of Bisha in KSA and got Her BaSc in Computerized information system. Because she always seeks to develop herself, Maram interned at social initiative called Wahaej for six months as a full stack developer, learned how to work with a great team in Agile methodology and her passion for UX/UI design started from there, She wants to learn more about users and how to help to build effective solutions for them, She was fortunate to have opportunity to learn from experts in Ironhack UX/UI bootcamp and currently she still learn more from them.
